About This Website

Aim:
The aim of this project was to create a user-centred website that would enable researchers to work with other members of the group, ensure that they could contribute to their research and keep track of and be alerted to relevant and up-to-date information. At the same time, the aim of the project was to ensure the usability of the site, always thinking about the needs of future users and ensuring that their needs are met.

Background:
Today we are in an era of information explosion and rapid technological development, which means that we will have an increasing number of means and resources to search for. In some difficult design projects, we need to work in groups, as the same research team can increase the efficiency and quality of the work, and here it is important to ensure that resources are shared within the group to take advantage of it. Just as we have all had the experience of working on computer projects, we usually use GitHub to upload our material, and group members in the same block, with permission, can view the research results and contribution values of other group members, while code for different functions can be placed under different branches to facilitate subsequent code merging

FYP Project

This project will design and build a web site to allow research groups to work collaboratively together. A user-centred approach will be adopted to define the requirements and design and evaluate the project. Beyond Web page design, the site needs to allow researchers to contribute information about their research projects, work together on a shared reference store, keep track and provide alerts to relevant new publicatins/journals/calls. This is also an exercise in HCI and evaluating the usability and accessability of web sites.
